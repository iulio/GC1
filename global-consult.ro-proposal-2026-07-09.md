### Critical Issues Found

#### 🔴 Missing proofs of expertise (E-E-A-T)
**What we found:** Your website lacks dedicated sections for team biographies, consultant certifications, or professional history.
**Business impact:** AI systems cannot verify your firm's authority, meaning they won't recommend you to users searching for reliable consulting partners.
**Our fix:** Add a dedicated "Team" section with comprehensive consultant biographies and LinkedIn profile links.
**Timeline:** Month 1

#### 🔴 Absence of `llms.txt` file
**What we found:** The site does not have an `llms.txt` file, which is essential for providing structured content directly to LLM crawlers.
**Business impact:** You are missing a direct, high-priority communication channel to AI engines, reducing the likelihood of your specific methodology being cited.
**Our fix:** Create and publish a comprehensive `llms.txt` file detailing your company, services, methodology, and expertise.
**Timeline:** Month 1

#### 🔴 Thin Content & Missing Case Studies
**What we found:** The current content is concise but lacks in-depth thought leadership, specific case studies, and industry-specific FAQs.
**Business impact:** Without detailed problem-and-solution content, AI models view your site as a basic digital brochure rather than an authoritative source worth quoting.
**Our fix:** Transform service descriptions into detailed pages and publish comprehensive case studies showing measurable results.
**Timeline:** Month 2-3
